<Note>
    Accessing Storage requires a paid subscription.
</Note>

VectorShift supports two forms of permanent storage: [Files](/vectorshift/platform/storage/files) and [Vector Stores](/vectorshift/platform/storage/vector-stores). You can use Files and Vector Stores in your Pipelines to provide context to your LLMs, significantly extending their capabilities.
