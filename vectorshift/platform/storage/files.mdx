---
title: "Files"
description: "Upload files and store outputs from Pipelines."
---

<Note>
    Accessing Storage requires a paid subscription.
</Note>

You can view your Files under the Files sub-tab in the Storage tab. Here, you can upload files to be utilized in Pipelines. To upload a File, click the "Add" button.

![](/images/image.png)

Utilize Files in your Pipelines with the [File node](/vectorshift/platform/pipelines/file). You can also save Pipeline outputs to your Files by using a [File Save node](/vectorshift/platform/pipelines/file#file-save-nodes).
