---
title: "Notion"
description: "Connect your Notion pages as data source."
---

You can set up a Notion integration by going to the Integrations tab, adding a new Integration, and selecting "Notion" as the Integration type. Next, you will need to choose pages that you want to connect with VectorShift.

![](/images/notion-gif.gif)

Integration with Notion

To use your connected pages, go create a pipeline and drag "`Integration`", choose "`Notion`" and choose "Read Data" for the function. The figure below is an example of how you can integrate Notion with your pipeline.

![](/images/21.png)

Visit the pipeline in the marketplace via this [link](https://app.vectorshift.ai/marketplace?pipeline%5Fid=64e216ea94dda4c4053dc26c).
