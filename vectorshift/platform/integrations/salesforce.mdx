---
title: "Salesforce"
description: "Query your Salesforce data from within your Pipelines."
---

You can set up a Salesforce integration by going to the Integrations tab, adding a new Integration, and selecting "Salesforce" as the Integration type. To complete your Salesforce integration, you will need to provide your Salesforce username, password, and security token:

![](/images/image.png)

After you have defined your Integration, you can access it from within your Pipelines using the Integration node:

![](/images/salesforce-demo.gif)
