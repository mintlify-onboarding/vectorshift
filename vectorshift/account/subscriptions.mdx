---
title: "Subscriptions"
---

Purchasing a subscription unlocks access to more features and free monthly [Credits](/vectorshift/account/credits).

All Personal accounts start with a Free subscription, granting access to the following features:

* Up to 5 pipeline runs per day.

* Up to 1 custom pipeline.

* Full marketplace access.

* 1 free credit on signup

To access additional features, you must upgrade to either a [Personal](/vectorshift/account/subscriptions/personal-subscriptions) or [Organization](/vectorshift/account/subscriptions/organization-subscriptions) subscription.
