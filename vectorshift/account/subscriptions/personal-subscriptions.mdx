title: "Personal Subscriptions"
description: "Personal subscriptions enable the use of additional features with limited sharing capabilities."
---

### Purchase a New Personal Subscription

To purchase a Personal subscription, take the following steps:

1. Navigate to "Settings".  

![](/images/27.gif)

2. Click "Upgrade" in the "Subscription" section, and select a plan.  

![](/images/28.gif)

### Manage an Existing Personal Subscription

To manage an existing subscription, take the following steps:

1. Navigate to "Settings".

2. Click "Manage" under the "Subscription" section.

3. Make any desired changes.
