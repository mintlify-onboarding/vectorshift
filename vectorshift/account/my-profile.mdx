### Navigate to My Profile

You can reach your profile by clicking on your user icon in the top-left of the screen and selecting "My Profle":

![](/images/vectorshift/account/2.gif)

From this section, you can view/change your username, upload a profile picture, update your first/last name, and create a new Organization.
